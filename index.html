From courtroom to canvas to literature, Evelyn enjoys analyzing the structure, design, and pattern of seemingly ordinary things; what makes one idea or presentation more appealing than another. Her specialty is discovering what users truly want and need through user research, especially if the user’s problems stem from structural issues and could be resolved with a more intuitive path for the user to take. Evelyn’s skills include, but aren’t limited to, using Figma, thinking outside-the-box, analyzing, and effectively interviewing potential users.
